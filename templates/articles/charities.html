Climate Change Charities

Looking to help by donating to a climate change charity? Read this article to gain some insight into some of our top picks.

Rainforest Foundation UK

Rainforest Foundation UK is a charity dedicated to saving rainforests localy and globally. They challenge logging companies, help forest communities gain land rights and help them manage their forests - enabling us to protect the environment. They believe the best way to combat deforestation is via empowering indigenous people to defend their land.

You can donate to Rainforest Foundation UK by clicking here.

Climate Emergency Fund

Climate Emergency Fund is a youth-based activist foundation created in 2019. Since then it has been taking the climate movement by storm with their protests and determinism. They seek a change in the way companies and countries have been destroying the planet immediately and believe we cannot wait any longer.

You can donate to Climate Emergency Fund by clicking here.

TerraPraxis

TerraPraxis is a UK-based nonprofit organisation aiming to find new and innovative ways to combat the energy crisis currently fueling climate change. They mainly focus on pushing nuclear power, which has been proven to be a lot safer than many think. They want to push the change to nuclear power in the UK as seen in other European countries such as Sweden and France.

You can donate to TerraPraxis by clicking here.

Clean Air Task Force

Clean Air Task Force is a charity which focuses on providing science and evidence-backed solutions to complex real world issues. They understand that the climate crisis is a big and complicated problem and a great challenge, and so explore every opportunity that shows promise. They have been operating for over 25 years.

You can donate to Clean Air Task Force by clicking here.
